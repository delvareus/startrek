Little Green Men      
 
On their way to drop off Nog at Starfleet Academy, Quark, Rom, and Nog are thrown back in time to 1947 Roswell, New Mexico.
     
🕖
Time Travel episode
🌎
Episode takes place on Earth
   
 
Thoughts: This episode is usually rated by others as being above-average, and I think it's largely because everyone loves a good time-travel Trek story. I'm going against the grain by only giving it 2 stars... but this one just wasn't for me. It's a little too goofy and there some very cringy moments involving oomox. And I'm pretty sure I got emphysema just by watching this episode. Of all the time-travel episodes in Trek, I'd rather watch any of them over this one.
   
Watch Recommendation: If you want to see a time-travel episode placed in 1947, don't let my rating stop you. I'm marking this as "Optional."
