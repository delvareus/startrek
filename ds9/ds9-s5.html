Doctor Bashir, I Presume

🔀
Crossover: Lewis Zimmerman and the Mark 1 EMH (VOY)
🙄
Annoying Bashir Moment: I mean... honestly, mostly the entire episode.
   
Watch Recommendation: I'm trying not to have too many episodes rated "Must Watch" so this one is only "Recommended." It's not one of my favorites, but there is a pretty imporant REVEAL that you should probably know about. So give it a watch if you want to see some Lewis Zimmerman crossover, or you can just keep yourself up-to-date by revealing this spoiler...
Spoiler » Starfleet discovers that Dr. Bashir had been genetically altered as a child to increase his physical and intellectual prowess. Since genetic manipulation is illegal in the Federation (think Khan Noonian Singh), Bashir's career was at risk. A deal was struck where his father would go to prison for two years and Bashir could remain in Starfleet. (We will never see Bashir's parents again.)
